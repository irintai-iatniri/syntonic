## Analysis

### 1. Entropy (Disorder)
- **Diffusion** maximizes entropy (H → 1.0), representing 'Heat Death' or maximum uniformity.
- **Delta** minimizes entropy (H = 0), representing a crystal or singularity.
- **DHSR** maintains **intermediate entropy** (H ≈ 0.5-0.7). It structures information without freezing it, characteristic of life/intelligence.

### 2. LMC Complexity (Structure)
- Standard measure of 'Complexity' = Entropy × Disequilibrium.
- **Diffusion**: Zero complexity (H high, D zero).
- **Delta**: Zero complexity (H zero, D high).
- **DHSR**: **Highest Complexity**. It balances order and chaos, residing at the 'Edge of Chaos'.
