## The Information Unit

Every unit of information is simultaneously three values:

| Value | Type | Domain |
|-------|------|--------|
| **WU** | Always = 1 | Whole unit (invariant) |
| **Mv** | Real number < 1 | Manifold value in M⁴ spacetime |
| **Tv** | Complex number < 1 | Toroidal value in T⁴ Hilbert space |

WU is the identity — always exactly 1, invariant across all transformations.

Mv exists in M⁴ spacetime. It is a real number, always less than 1.

Tv exists in T⁴ Hilbert space. It is a complex number, always less than 1.

Every information unit carries all three simultaneously. They are not three descriptions of one thing — they are three aspects that exist together.

## The DHSR Cycle

Information flows through four discrete stages:

### D (Differentiation)

Division of a whole unit into many whole units.

The WU divides via differentiation into X, where X = units with varying Mv (which are equal to WU) and Tv (which are equal to potential states on the Golden Lattice intersection of the Golden Light Cone).

This is expansion into novelty. One becomes many. Each child unit has its own Mv and Tv values.

### H (Harmonization)

Recombination of whole units into ratio pairs.

X then moves into pairs of whole value units. The Mv and Tv of the units of each H create a ratio:

$$X_1(Mv:Tv) : X_2(Mv:Tv) \rightarrow DH$$

The Mv and Tv then move into pairs of like values:
- X₁(Mv₁:Mv₂) 
- X₂(Tv₁:Tv₂)

This creates 2 new DH pairs — one pair of real numbers and one pair of complex numbers.

The ratio of DH is equal to (Mv₁:Mv₂):(Tv₁:Tv₂).

### S (Syntonization)

A process of oscillation between valid Mv pairs and Tv pairs.

This is where testing against φ occurs:

- If Mv = φ → proceed to R (recursion begins)
- If DH < φ → accumulate (gain a new X)
- If DH > φ → each DH becomes a WU and D recurs (split again)

The best knot for the topology is the one that wins. This is not random selection — it is geometric fit. The topology determines which configuration passes.

### R (Recursion)

A process of WU filtering and recycling.

When R begins, subtract the amount of WU from the total Mv (which should equal 0.618).

The syntonized information (SiU) moves to the center of the torus.

The WU is split into:
- Mv₁ = 1 → this is a WU (continues)
- Mv₂ = 0.618 → this becomes a new WU (nascent)

**Critical:** The cycle's Tv phase becomes permanent in T⁴. This is not temporary storage — it is permanent record.

Syntony is not a value. **Syntony is the oscillation itself.**

0.618 for novelty (or 1.618 for the whole) is the maximum amount of information that can make it through the aperture during the recursion cycle. The rest is recycled as entropy (de-cohered information) for the next cycle.

Syntony is an island of stability that continues cycles.

### The Boundaries

There are two extremes that terminate the cycle:

$$S_{min} = D_{max} \rightarrow \text{complete decoherence}$$

This is chaos. All structure dissolves. Too much differentiation without harmonization.

$$S_{max} = H_{max} \rightarrow \text{crystallization}$$

This is frozen. All motion stops. Too much harmonization without differentiation.

**Both extremes stop the cycle.** Information requires flow. Stagnant value is lost value. The island of stability exists only through continuous oscillation between these extremes.
